Data Analytics and Machine Learning

This course will introduce essential principles and concepts in data analytics and machine learning through a series of lectures, and learners will apply the concepts through hands-on workshops using either RapidMiner or Google Colab (Python). The students will be applying these statistics and data modelling techniques in practical projects, through which they will learn how to develop real world analytics solutions using RapidMiner.
