Data Analytics and Machine Learning

This course will introduce essential principles and concepts in data analytics and machine learning through a series of lectures, and students will apply the concepts through hands-on workshops using either RapidMiner or Google Colab (Python). The students will also apply these techniques to a real world scenario of their choice in a mini-project.

Prerequisite: Nil

Schedule

14 March - Introduction, Descriptive Statistics & Data Visualization
15 March - Supervised Learning (Regression)
16 March - Supervised Learning (Classification)

20 March - Unsupervised Learning 21 March - Text Analytics
